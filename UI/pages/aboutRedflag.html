What is Broadcaster?

Corruption is a huge bane to Africaâ€™s development. African countries must develop novel and localized solutions that will curb this menace, hence the birth of Broadcaster. Broadcaster enables any/every citizen to bring any form of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public. You can also report on things that need government intervention .

What is a Redflag?

A Broadcaster redflag in a report made by a citizen aiming to bring any form of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public.

What is an Intervention?

A Broadcaster Intervention in a report made by a citizen aiming to shed light on things that need government intervention.
